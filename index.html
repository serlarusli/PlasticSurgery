Here's a look at cosmetic plastic surgery trends around the world...


According to International Society of Aesthetics Plastic Surgery statistics, the US topped the chart in the total number of plastic surgery with more than 1.4 million procedures performed in 2015. However, when the number of population is taken into account, South Korea had the highest number of plastic surgery procedures per capita (close to 0.009 per person).

The survey looks at the number of plastic surgeries performed in nine countries in 2015.

Plastic surgery in USA


Breast augmentation was the most popular plastic surgery procedure in the US, with more than 300,000 procedures performed in 2015. Liposuction comes next with close to 250,000 procedures performed.


Source: International Society of Aesthetics Plastic Surgery

Total spending


Americans spent more than USD 1 billion in 2015 on breast augmentation and rhinoplasty (nose job) each.

The total spending on cosmetic plastic surgery amounted to almost USD 7.5 billion, or around the size of the GDP of Kyrgyzstan in 2015.


Source: American Society of Plastic Surgeons
